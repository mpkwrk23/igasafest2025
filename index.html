Imam Gazzali Academy Students' Association (IGASA)

The Imam Ghazzali Academy Students Association (IGASA) is the student organization of Imam Ghazzali Academy, which is affiliated with the Wayanad Muslim Orphanage. For the past 20 years, IGASA has been dedicated to promoting the academic, extracurricular, artistic, and athletic development of the students at our institution. Our goal is to foster personal growth and uphold our steadfast religious values within the community.

Igasa Fest 2k25

Igasa Fest is an annual arts festival held for the students of Imam Gazzali Academy. The event is officially sanctioned and supported by the academy to promote and develop educational activities among students. Recognized for enhancing skills, extracurricular activities, creative thinking, and productive interests, the festival includes skill-oriented and talent-based contests and programs. It plays a crucial role in the development of students at Imam Gazzali Academy and is an essential part of its curriculum..
